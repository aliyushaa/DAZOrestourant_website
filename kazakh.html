DAZO: Kazakh couisine


The menu

Baursak

soft cottage cheese is a key component added to the dough, ending up with crispy skinned tender balls

Quyrdak

is roast meat, lots of onions and vegetables.By-products are used from meat,traditional potatoes, carrots, onions, pumpkin.

Kespe

is homemade noodles, served both as a main dish with a large piece of meat and vegetables, and as a side dish, without meat and.

Beshbarbak

The visiting card of a feast in Kazakhstan is Beshbarmak. This is a very ancient dish consisting of pieces of boiled lamb, broth and pieces of dough.

Palau

The main dish that Kazakhs eat on weekdays and on major holidays is Palau. Ingredients : rice and meat. Believe it or not, this dish is more than 2 thousand years old.

Manty

Manty is a steamed dish of finely chopped meat and thinly rolled dough. There are several recipes for making manti, and the secrets of originality are in minced meat.
